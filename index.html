Wedding Details

Our Ceremony venue is the Madrone Grove in Roberts Regional Park.
We chose this because it's a beautiful and special area for us that is next to fun, curvy roads that we ride our turbo-charged subaru on as well as our motorycles. This park is full of California's famous redwoods and is 15 minutes away from our Reception.

Our Reception venue is Piedmont Community Center.
We chose this because it is in a historical part of the East Bay, a city within the city of Oakland. This building is simple and elegant and is set in a park full of history and old growth trees.

Our Caterer is Keena's Kitchen
We chose Chef Keena for her dedication to seasonal, sustainable, farm-direct sourcing. We will provide an omnivores feast: suitible for meatists and vegans alike.

Travel Tips

Here are two very helpful websites

Kayak.com for Travel booking and Flight,Hotel and Rental Car Pricing for all services providers
AirBnB.com for independent bed and breakfast booking

For those of you driving -

Google Maps can be used to plan a route
Weather Underground can be used to confirm weatherconditions on route.

  • Do I need to rent a car ?

    It depends.
    You can use the BART to get to from both SFO and OAK to Oakland, San Francisco, Berkeley. Visit BART.gov for info.
    Lyft, Uber and taxis are prominent here.
    Consider bicycle rentals for fun in SF!

  • Where should I stay ?

    Again, it depends on how long your trip is and what you'd like to see. Contact either Karleen or Curtis if you need help. We strongly recomend staying in Oakland or Berekely the night of the wedding. Transbay transit stops at 11 PM. Check out AirBNB in the Lake Merrit/Grand Lake, Temescal, Rockridge, Piedmont areas of Oakland. Lake Merrit/Grand Lake is near our home and has a Saturday farmer's market, dining, shops, and parks. The Berkeley and Oakland Hills are gorgeous and in the vincinity of both venues. Oakland Hotels, Berekely Hotels are the second best options. Again, please reach out if you need extra help.

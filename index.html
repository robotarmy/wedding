Wedding Details

Our Ceremony venue is the Madrone Grove in Roberts Regional Park.

We chose this because it's a beautiful and special area for us that is next to fun, curvy roads that we ride our turbo-charged subaru on as well as our motorycles. This park is full of California's famous redwoods.

Our Reception venue is Piedmont Community Center.

We chose this because it is in a historical part of the East Bay, a city within the city of Oakland. This building is simple and elegant and is set in a park full of history and old growth trees.

Our Caterer is Keena's Kitchen

We chose Chef Keena for her dedication to seasonal, sustainable, farm-direct sourcing. We will provide an omnivores feast: suitible for meatists and vegans alike.
